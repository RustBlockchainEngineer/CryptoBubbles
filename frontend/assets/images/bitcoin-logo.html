Bitcoin logo

First appeared on 31 October 2008, Bitcoin is an innovative payment network and a new kind of money, an open source P2P money.

Bitcoin.org logo

Developered by Satoshi Nakamoto and Martti Malmi, Bitcoin uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority or banks; managing transactions and the issuing of bitcoins is carried out collectively by the network. Bitcoin is open-source; its design is public, nobody owns or controls Bitcoin and everyone can take part.
